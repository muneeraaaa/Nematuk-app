Compliances & Regulations

Stay compliant, stay sustainable

UAE Policies

National framework for food safety

Food Regulations

The UAE enforces strict laws on food storage, handling, and distribution to protect consumers and ensure sustainability. Businesses must register products, comply with labelling, and follow proper storage guidelines.

These policies aim to reduce waste and safeguard public health.

Food Shelf Life

Expiry rules and storage standards

Expiry Guidelines

Food products must display clear expiry or best-before dates. Businesses are responsible for ensuring expired food is not sold or donated. Proper storage extends shelf life and minimizes waste.

Carbon Labels

Making food's footprint visible

Environmental Impact

Carbon labels display the CO₂ emissions linked to a product's production and transportation. The goal is to help consumers make sustainable choices and encourage businesses to lower emissions.

Safe Delivery

Food safety on the move

Delivery Protocols

Food delivery businesses must maintain safe temperatures, use hygienic packaging, and train drivers in handling practices. These rules protect food safety and consumer trust.

Composting Policies

Turning waste into resource

Waste Management

The UAE encourages composting of food scraps to reduce landfill pressure and generate natural fertilizers. Businesses can adopt composting systems or partner with municipal programs.

Registering Fees

Licensing and compliance costs

Business Requirements

Food businesses must pay registration and licensing fees, which vary by emirate and business size. These fees ensure compliance monitoring and food safety enforcement.

Advertising

Responsible food marketing

Marketing Guidelines

Food advertising must be truthful, not misleading, and must not promote unsafe or expired products. The UAE monitors food promotions to protect consumers and promote healthy lifestyles.

Sustainability Measures

Greener food systems

Eco Practices

Businesses are encouraged to adopt recycling, reduce plastic use, and manage energy efficiently. These measures align with the UAE's Net Zero 2050 goals.

About Us

Because every plate matters.

Ne'matuk

At Ne'matuk, we believe food is a blessing (*ni'mah*) that should be valued, not wasted. Across the UAE, thousands of tons of food end up in landfills every year, harming the environment and wasting precious resources. We are here to change that.

To fight food waste and turn surplus into impact — empowering households, businesses, and communities to share more and waste less.

Our Vision

Our Impact
